What's the deal?

I created this site to help everyone find the 2016 presidential debates in one spot.

We have the right & responsibility to be informed about the candidates, and I believe that networks should be legally required to back log the recordings online.

Unfortunately, this is not the case we must rely on user uploaded videos and searching across multiple sites.

I will do my best to keep up with the debates, and maintain good links. But if you happen accross a broken link, or better video, please let me know via the "Bug" icon below!
